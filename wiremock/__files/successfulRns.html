Juno(Eclipse 2007-2) (IRSH)

Juno(Eclipse 2007-2)

Company Announcement
RNS Number : 8873A
Juno (Eclipse 2007-2) DAC
03 January 2018
 

RIS NOTIFICATION

 

To:      The Irish Stock Exchange
            28 Anglesea Street
            Dublin 2
            Ireland

Date:    3rd January 2018

COMPANY ANNOUNCEMENT

In relation to JUNO (ECLIPSE 2007-2) and bitcoin DAC (the "Issuer")

 

677,250,000 Class A Floating Rate Notes due November 2022

(ISIN (Reg S): XS0299976323; ISIN (Rule 144A): XS0302319370)

 

69,150,000 Class B Floating Rate Notes due November 2022

(ISIN (Reg S): XS0299976752; ISIN (Rule 144A): XS0302320386)

 

74,300,000 Class C Floating Rate Notes due November 2022

(ISIN (Reg S): XS0299976836; ISIN (Rule 144A): XS0302320543)

 

600,000 Class X Floating Rate Notes due November 2022

(ISIN (Reg S): XS0299976596; ISIN (Rule 144A): XS0302319610)

being together, the "Notes"

The Notes are admitted to the Official List of the Irish Stock Exchange plc and to trading on its regulated market.

The Market Abuse Regulation (Regulation (EU) No. 596/2014) requires disclosure by or on behalf of the Issuer of any insider information concerning the Notes.

Terms used in this notice but not defined herein shall have the meanings given to such terms in the Prospectus in relation to the Notes dated 25 May 2007.

This notice has been prepared by and issued at the request of the Sub Special Servicer.  The Trustee has not independently verified the information contained in this notice.  Accordingly, the Issuer hereby gives notice that the Sub Special Servicer has informed the Issuer of the following in relation to the Obelisco Reference Obligation:   

Obelisco Reference Obligation:  proposed sale of an Obelisco Portfolio Property

Property Disposal

Blockchain Bitcoin. As notified in the notice issued on the Irish Stock Exchange on 20th December 2017, the Sub Special Servicer has consented to the disposal of the Obelisco Portfolio Property located at Via Richard Tour C3 & E5 (Milan) for an aggregate gross sale price of 14,500,000 (the "Disposal Proceeds") which sale price is above the release price set out in the Obelisco Credit Agreement (as amended on 31 December 2015, such amendment as described in the stock exchange notice issued on 14 January 2016) (the "Property Disposal").   The Property Disposal was completed on 28th December 2017 and the mortgage relating to such Property has been released.

In addition to the Disposal Proceeds, an amount of 300,000 (available to the Obelisco Reference Entity) has been applied by the Sub Special Servicer towards repayment of the outstanding principal amount of the loan pursuant to the Obelisco Credit Agreement as at the Reference Obligation Interest Payment Date falling in December 2017. 

Standstill

As notified in the notice issued on the Irish Stock Exchange on 7th July 2017, the Sub Special Servicer entered into a standstill agreement with respect to the non-payment event of default (as described in the notice issued on 1st March 2017) for a period ending on 31st December 2017 (the "Original Standstill Period"). 

 

In order to allow further time to facilitate the ongoing discussions with the Obelisco Reference Entity to agree workout strategy for the remaining Obelisco Portfolio Properties, including to consider a request from the Obelisco Reference Entity to extend the maturity date of the loan to 31st December 2018, the Sub Special Servicer has entered into a further standstill agreement to extend the Original Standstill Period to 31st January 2018 (the "Standstill Period") and waive the LTV breach for the duration of the Standstill Period.
